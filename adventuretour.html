Adventure Tour

15 Nights & 16 Days.
Price -
1-2 Pax USD 1260 per each
3-6 pax USD 1080 per each
7 & above USD 990 per each

Day 01 - Arrival at Katunayake International Airport. Drive to Mt.Lavinia
Day 02 - Drive to Kithulgala. Spend the night in Eco Lodges. Remember the film “The Bridge over the River Kwai” bridge for the film was set here. White water rafting and trekking.
Day 03 - Horton Plains Nature Reserve, located on Sri Lanka’s highest plateau. Over night stay. Climb the worlds end to explore the most spectacular view in Sri Lanka.
Day 04 - Drive to Nuwara Eliya and relax for a day.
Day 05 - Visit a tea factory and observe how tea is made. To Ella by train or Vehicle.
Day 06 - Ella has the most beautiful views, you could find in Sri Lanka. Night in Ella.
Day 07 - To Uda Walawe. Afternoon Safari at the park. Overnight in Uda Walawe
Day 08 - Arugam Bay - Dive, surf and relax in ArugamBay. ArugamBay is on the list of the top ten surf points in the world. Pottuvil Point is every surfers dream tropical wave
Day 09 - V Visit Pottuvil Lagoon. It is a magical place of mangrovers & birds. Over night in ArugamBay.
Day 10 - To Yala – Afternoon Safari and rest in Yala
Day 11 - To Via Bundala, Hambantota to Unwatuna.
Day 12 - Visit the stilt fisherman of Weligama, the Galle Fort and the Turtle hatchery.
Day 13 - Relax at Beach resort.
Day 14 - Relax at Beach resort.
Day 15 - Transfer to Mout Lavinia. City tour/Shopping
Day 16 - Airport transfer or continue tour North Central and Central


Accommodation : Standard double with breakfast

Transport : A/C car with English speaking Guide/driver.

Entrance fee : All entrance fees will have to be paid by you.
