Grand Miracle Tour

17 Nights & 18 Days.
Price -
1-2 Pax USD 1260 per each
3-6 pax USD 1080 per each
7 & above USD 990 per each

Day 01 - Meet and transfer to Hotel in Kalpitiya, and relax on the beach. Night in Kalpitiya.
Day 02 - Dolphin watch Cruz and proceed to Anuradhapura and visit all ruin sites and proceed to Habarana for the night.
Day 03 - Climb Sigiriya Lion Rock in the morning and afternoon visit to Aukkana and back to the same hotel.
Day 04 - Visit Dambulla Rock Temple and drive to Nilaveeli. Spend the evening on the beach.
Day 05 - Day out to Pigeon Island and evening visits to Fort Frederick, Swami Rock and 9 wells. Back to the same hotel.
Day 06 - Drive to Polonnaruwa and visit the Ruin City and relax the evening in Polonnaruwa.
Day 07 - Drive to Passikuda beach and proceed to Potuwil.
Day 08 - Relax on the beach and afternoon visit sites around Arugambay and evening boat ride on the Pottuvil Lagoon. Night at the same hotel.
Day 09 - Spend the morning on the beach and proceed to Tissamaharama, optional visit to Kataragama Temple. Night in Tissa.
Day 10 - Explore the Yala National park. Jeep Safari and travel to Ella. Ella town is the nights, bit of a happening with foreigners. Night in Ella.
Day 11 - Optional walk up to the Small Adams Peak and proceed to Haputhale and visit Adisham Bungalow. Night in Haputhale.
Day 12 - Visit Lipton seat and Bambarakanda Falls and proceed to Castelreigh Lake Cottages via Maskeliya. Night in Dick-Oya.
Day 13 - Relax the morning at the Lake view cottage and proceed to Kithulgala. Experience a level 4 white water rafting at the Kelani River where the Oscar award winning movie "Bridge of the River Kwai" was filmed. Proceed to Nuwara Eliya for the night.
Day 14 - Drive up to Horton Plain Rain Forest and walk up to the worlds end. Relax and proceed to Kandy. Evening Cultural Dance at 5.30 p.m., and evening visit to the Temple of the Tooth. Night in Kandy.
Day 15 - Visit Royal Botanical Garden, Spice Garden, Pinnawela Elephant Orphanage and proceed to Unawatuna.
Day 16 & 17 - Relax on the beach in Unawatuna
